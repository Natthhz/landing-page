About Shiina Mahiru

Shiina Mahiru is the main heroine of "The Angel Next Door Spoils Me Rotten." Despite her cold exterior at school, she's a caring and gentle person who excels at housework. After helping her neighbor Amane during his illness, she begins to develop a special bond with him while maintaining her "perfect girl" reputation at school.
